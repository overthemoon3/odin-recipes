Chipotle Chickpea Sandwich

Home Page

Description

Now, this sandwich has been my go-to meal for the past two years here at UCSC. For one, it's one of the cheapest things being offered at around $6, and it keeps me full for hours!

I don't actually know the official recipie but I'm going to be making it up. I mean it's just a sandwich, and I can clearly see what they put in it. So, it shouldn't be that far off, right?

Ingredients

  • telera roll
  • tomato slices
  • onion slices
  • spring mix
  • canned chickpeas
  • chipotle sauce
  • salt, pepper, other seasoning (still figuring this one out)

Steps

  1. take out the canned chickpeas and give them a thorough wash
  2. set them aside in a bowl after soaking up as much water as possible using a paper towel
  3. cut up onion and tomato slices if you haven't already
  4. get the salad out
  5. set the telera roll in front of you
  6. lightly smash the chickpeas and add in chipotle sauce and seasoning
  7. assemble the sandwich by putting in the chickpeas, salad, onion, and tomato
  8. enjoy!
